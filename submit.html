Community contributions

Useful material deserves a clearer home.

Share notes, study guides, practice material, or carefully organized course resources. Every submission is manually reviewed before it can appear in the catalogue.

The review process

A simple route from contribution to catalogue.

  1. Submit the details

    Tell us the course, semester, topic, resource type, and provide the Google Drive link.

  2. We review the entry

    NexCore checks that the information is useful, clear, and appropriate to share.

  3. We publish the catalogue record

    Approved material receives a verified catalogue entry. The file stays in its original Google Drive location.
